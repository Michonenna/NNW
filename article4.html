A 42-YEAR-OLD man of Chililabombwe district on the Copperbelt province has been arrested for allegedly defiling a mentally disturbed woman.

Steven Mwansa is alleged to have defiled the victim after she was sent to charcoal at a nearby market.

Copperbelt province acting police commanding officer Tresphord Kasale confirmed the incident in a statement.

Mr Kasale said the incident happened yesterday around 09:00 hours in unfinished building along Independece road in Chililabombwe.

He said the victim’s mother reported that her daughter, aged 21, who is mentally retarded was allegedly defiled by a person only known as Steven Mwansa, aged 42 of house number E 320 PP Zambia Compound Chililabombwe.

“She was caused to sustain painful private parts and general body pains. The incident occurred when the mentally retarded victim was sent by her mother to go buy charcoal and she took long in coming back. That’s what prompted the mother to make a follow up to check up on her, as she was going along the road, she met up with a group of people who were holding her daughter on her hand and also holding unknown man,” he said.

Mr Kasale said the victim’s mother was then informed that the Mwansa was found having canal knowledge with her daughter.

He said the victims mother then explained the mental condition of her daughter and Mwansa was apprehended and reported to Chililabombwe police station.
